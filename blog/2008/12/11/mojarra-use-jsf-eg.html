Mojarra use on the JSF EG Blog

Dec 11, 2008

As I mention in every introductory JSF talk I give, Sun’sMojarra is the most popular implementation of JSF. Mojarra provides the JSF implementation for every major app server vendor except for Apache Geronimo, which uses Apache MyFaces. I recently took a poll of the JSF 2.0 expert group members regarding their use of Mojarra. I’ve collected the best of their responses in a little table on the Mojarra website, at <https://javaserverfaces.dev.java.net/customers/expert-group.html>. Though not a complete list of all EG members using Mojarra, it does give some insight into how the world’s top JSF experts feel about Mojarra.
