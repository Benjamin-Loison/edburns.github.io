Thanks to my mom, who held on to this VHS tape for 30 years.

You may ask, "Why not just upload this to YouTube?" I tried. The answer is that in 1990, when we made this video, there was no such thing as a "take down notice". So, I'm hosting it here (via GitHub Pages and DropBox) instead.

If the video does not play, it is because the DropBox redirect has changed and I haven't yet written the JavaScript code to resolve that. In the meantime, the dropbox share link is https://www.dropbox.com/s/raw/agyapcysraz4ygr/20200320-1990-coh-end-of-year-video.mp4.
